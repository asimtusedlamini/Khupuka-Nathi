Welcome to KHUPHUKA NATHI

Overview of our organisation

Nathi is the national nonprofit organization based in Pretoria, South Africa. In 2015, The company was founded with the aim to respond to the growing need for youth empowerment. Especially those that were previously underprivileged. young people in South Africa face a similar problem such as: Lack of access to quality education, Skills development programs, job assistance. The main goal of Khuphuka Nathi is to create structured paths that facilitate the progression of young people towards thriving careers and independence.

Our Mission

Khuphuka Nathi's mission is to empower young people by providing access to quality education, practical skills training, and economic opportunities. The organisation is committed to equipping youth with the knowledge, confidence, and resources needed to participate actively in the economy, improve their livelihoods, and contribute positively to their communities.

Raise awareness of activities and programs. Use online application forms to help the youth find educational support, Skills opportunities, stipends. Expose the youth to employment opportunities.
